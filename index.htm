A Digital Ecosystem for Well-being

MindWing is a digital ecosystem built on a single, efficient Core Digital Platform. This foundation provides essential tools to all users, while our specialized products—MindWing Student and MindWing Pro—offer customized features that address the unique barriers identified in our research.

Core Platform

Gamified Mindfulness

Wellness activities or challenges to earn rewards, and enter prize draws (e.g., vouchers from local businesses).

Seamless Integration

Login via university / work email. Collaboration with well-being teams / HR / NGOs. Custom analytics dashboards for monitoring anonymized trends.

Wellness Hub

On-demand library of expert-curated videos & playlists. Includes AI-driven content suggestions and live virtual sessions (Q&As, mindfulness) with recordings.

MindWing Student

Daily Mood Check-ins

A core feature for students to track emotional triggers and build self-awareness. Provides weekly insights and gentle nudges. If serious symptoms are identified, it triggers an alert for immediate intervention via our 3rd-party NGO partners.

Free Counselling Access

Direct connection to free professional help via university & NGO partners, removing the cost barrier.

MindWing Pro

Digital Emotion Mirror

AI-driven, discreet check-in helps recognise and manage stress early, designed for busy professionals.

Resonance Pulse

Neuroscience-based audio-vibrational reset for instant calm and enhanced focus via headphones.

Affordable Psychologist Appointments

Book confidential appointments with verified psychologists at affordable partner rates.

Clinical Rigor & Evidence Base

Our core platform and specialized features are developed under evidence-based psychological frameworks to ensure user safety and efficacy.

Daily Mood Check-ins (Student)

Based on: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps students track emotional triggers and behavioral patterns to increase self-awareness.

Gamified Mindfulness (Core)

Based on: Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)

Short, guided practices and social challenges designed for all users to promote self-regulation and stress reduction.

Digital Emotion Mirror (Pro)

Based on: Affective Computing & AI Psychology

AI-driven discreet check-ins for professionals to quickly recognize and manage stress and cognitive load.

Counselling Integration (Student & Pro)

Based on: Stepped Care Model

Directs students to free professional help via partners; provides affordable, verified psychologist appointments for professionals.
